Hello, my name is Tom Parham and I would be interested to in taking care of your pet. I have been a pet owner nearly all my life and truly understand just how important our pets are to us. Choosing the right person to care for your pet is very important. Leave Me at Home pet walking and pet setting is something I started because of the loss of my Weimaraner Lilly. After boarding Lilly at a kennel for an overnight stay, the following morning I received a call from the kennel advising me that she had passed away in the night. Lilly and I were inseparable, and I truly believe she passed away from the stress of not being home and being away from me. I was devastated and because of this loss I started my business so no one else would have to experience this same tragedy.
My schedule is very flexible and I have several references that I can give you as to the type of care I provide for your pet. You will find that I treat your pets as if they were my own. In addition, one of my clients is a Metro Police Officer!


Kind regards,
Tom Parham
